Data Mix Comparison
Accuracy and F1 Macro across three class balance conditions
Why Mix Matters
Balanced (1:1:1)
Equal samples per class. Prevents the model from ignoring minority classes. Best for macro F1.
Neutral-Heavy (1:3:1)
Oversample neutral class. Improves neutral recall but may hurt negative/positive precision.
Original Distribution
Natural class imbalance from the dataset. Most realistic but biases the model toward majority class.
